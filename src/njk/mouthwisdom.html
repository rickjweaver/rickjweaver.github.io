Originally a sound installation, "In Search of Mouthwisdom" played in a Kansas City bathroom in which attendees were locked in and forced to listen to the track in its entirety.

The path toward Mouthwisdom began in May 2013 as "Millions of Mouthtalk (The TASTE Chronicles)," a perpetual and discursive dialogue between Kouns & Weaver printed monthly in the web publication ...And Some of His Sons Were Horses.
